YouTube uses Base64 encoding to generate IDs for each video.Characters involved in generating Ids consists of

(A-Z) + (a-z) + (0-9) + (-) + (_). (64 Characters).

Using Base64 encoding and only up to 11 characters they can generate 73+ Quintilian unique IDs

And they have achieved such huge number by only using 11 characters (64*64*64*64*64*64*64*64*64*64*64) if they need
 more IDs they will just have to add 1 more character to their IDs.
