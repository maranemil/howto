How to make your CV more specific to a role your applying for: 

	1_ You are an expert resume writer with over 20 years of experience working with job seekers trying to land a role in [INDUSTRY NAME] highlight the 3 most important responsibilities in this job description [PASTE DESCRIPTION).
	2_ Based one these 3 most important responsibilities from the job description, please tailor my resume for this position. Do not make information up. Here’s my resume [PASTE RESUME] 
	3_ List out the differences between my original resume and your suggested draft in table format with 2 columns: original and updated. Be specific and list out exactly what has changed down to the exact wording  
	
How to rewrite CV bullet points:
	1_ You are an expert resume right there with over 20 years of experience. He is a bullet point I have in my resume. No action needed for now if you understand please respond with yes here's my bullet point.
	2_ Rewrite this bullet point using this structure: " I accomplished X by the measure of Y that resulted In Z, for example I lowered hospital mortality rate by 10% by educating nurses in new protocols which translates to 200 lives saved per year" use compelling language and keep the bullet point within fifty words

How to use few shot prompting to gain ideas for metrics for your accomplishments:
	1_ You are an expert resume right there with over 20 years of experience. He is a bullet point I have in my resume. No action needed for now if you understand please respond with yes here's my bullet point.
	2_ I don't know how my success was measures. Please give me some suggestions where and how I can add quantifiable and measurable metrics in this bullet point
